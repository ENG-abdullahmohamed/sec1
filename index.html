About Football

Football is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players with a spherical ball. It is played by over 250 million players in over 200 countries, making it the world's most popular sport.

The game is played on a rectangular field called a pitch with a goal at each end. The objective is to score by moving the ball beyond the goal line into the opposing goal.
